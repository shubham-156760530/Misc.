ITR Filing Procedure-

Visit : https://www.incometax.gov.in/iec/foportal/
Go to : Help section and Click on view all topic and select return/form applicable to me. There we can see which ITR type we need to file, for salaried employee we have to file ITR-1.
Create user ID for ITR filing.
Now do login-
Login Id: PAN CARD
After login verify your details.
Download form 16 and form26-AS (Form26 AS can be downloaded after login to income tax site). For downloading Form26-AS- go to e-File section and click on Income Tax Returns and then click on View Form 26AS and then select assesment year and select view as HTML and click on View/Downloads and then click on Export to PDF.
